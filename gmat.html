The GMAT Focus Edition
Three sections.
One strategy.

The GMAT Focus Edition replaced the classic GMAT in 2023. Our curriculum is built specifically around the new structure — with dedicated strategies for each section.

01
Quantitative Reasoning
21 Questions · 45 Minutes
Problem Solving only. Tests arithmetic, algebra, and higher-order reasoning. We cover Number Properties, Algebra, Word Problems, and Advanced Quant strategies with a focus on accuracy under time pressure.
02
Verbal Reasoning
23 Questions · 45 Minutes
Critical Reasoning and Reading Comprehension. We train logical argument analysis, inference identification, and structured reading — skills that improve both your score and your MBA application essays.
03
Data Insights
20 Questions · 45 Minutes
The newest section: Data Sufficiency, Multi-Source Reasoning, Table Analysis, Graphics Interpretation, and Two-Part Analysis. We build the structured thinking this section demands from the ground up.
