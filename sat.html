The Digital SAT
Two sections.
One score.

The Digital SAT replaced the paper SAT in 2024. It is shorter, adaptive, and computer-based — and requires a very different preparation strategy. Our curriculum is built specifically for this new format.

01
Reading and Writing
54 Questions · 64 Minutes · 2 Modules
Evidence-based reading, grammar and usage, and vocabulary-in-context. The Digital SAT's adaptive engine means your second module adjusts to your first — we train you to perform consistently across all difficulty levels.
  • Craft and Structure — vocabulary, text structure, cross-text connections
  • Information and Ideas — central ideas, inferences, command of evidence
  • Standard English Conventions — boundaries, form, sense, agreement
  • Expression of Ideas — transitions, rhetorical synthesis
02
Math
44 Questions · 70 Minutes · 2 Modules
Algebra, Advanced Math, Problem Solving, Geometry, and Trigonometry. The Digital SAT allows a calculator throughout — but speed and accuracy still matter. We build systematic problem-solving habits, not just formula recall.
  • Algebra — linear equations, inequalities, systems of equations
  • Advanced Math — quadratics, polynomials, exponential functions
  • Problem Solving & Data Analysis — ratios, rates, statistics, probability
  • Geometry & Trigonometry — area, volume, circles, right triangles

Score range: 400 – 1600  ·  Reading & Writing: 200–800  ·  Math: 200–800  ·  Duration: 2 hrs 14 min  ·  Format: Adaptive (Bluebook app)
